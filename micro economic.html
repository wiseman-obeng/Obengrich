The Economic Problem

Scarcity: this is when resource is available but limited in supply to satisfy all human wants.it is the fundamental problem because human being is associated with unlimited want of which these scares resource is not enough to satisfy them. This result in the allocation of resources to meet our unlimited wants and needs. The problem give birth to choose, opportunity cost, trade off etc.

Positive statement attempt to describe what happen and why it happened .it is testable statement which means it can be prove and disprove whiles Normative statement is statement which attempt to prescribe what should be done. it cannot be proved or disprove.

Examples of positive statement

  1. High government budget deficits will cause rates to increase.
  2. The tax revenues from of selling education materials are worth enough to increase the nation income.

Examples of normative statements

  1. The inflation rate should be low.
  2. tertiary education ought to be free.
